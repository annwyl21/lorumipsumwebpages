Baby Birds are Super Cute

Baby robin sitting on my garden gate in Spring 2021.
In Spring 2021 the robins had a clutch of tiny blue eggs in my nest box and this fluffy baby robin emerged to learn about life in my garden nursery. He or she (since it is hard to tell with robins) had a lovely start pulling up worms and chasing baby blue tits about.

Fledgling great tit sitting in the pear tree.
In Spring 2021, there were alot of baby birds in my garden and this tiny, fluffy great tit played happily in my pear tree. He was easy to identify from the blue tits because I can see his black tie developing down his tummy and it goes all the way down his tummy through his legs so that tells me he is a male.

The robin grew and became more curious.
